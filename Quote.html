What happens next...

You fill out the form.

You're not putting your information into a huge database.  It goes straight to our office and we go to work for your ministry.

We follow up with you

Agent Robin Pickett will call or email you (whichever you prefer) to get the ball rolling.  

We meet at your location

Robin will come to your ministry location at a time that's convenient for you.  He will need just 30 minutes of your time to gather some information, and then he will take pictures and measurements.

We will analyze your ministry's specific exposures and needs and crunch the numbers to shop for the best coverage at a competitive premium.  

We present your quote

Robin will send you a quote with detailed coverage explanations so you know exactly what you're getting.  To accomodate you, he can also come present the proposal to your board in person.

You're covered

We can bind coverage right when you need it.  Just say the word, and you're covered.  Your quote is valid for 60 days to give you time to make decisions.
